Grilled Cheese

Contrary to what one might imagine, a 'grilled cheese' is not simply a block of cheese that is cooked on a grill. Although this dish is not quite that simple, it is not much more complicated. At it's core, a grilled cheese consists of cheese enclosed by bread that is then toasted.

At it's core, the grilled cheese is a dish that is lauded for it's simple, but wholesome nature. However, that is not to say that this dish cannot be elevated. Through a careful selection of bread, cheese(s) and accoutrements, this dish can go from a simple snack to a hearty meal.

Ingredients for a simple Grilled cheese

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Oil or butter

Steps to make a common Cheese Pizza

  1. Put cheese between two slices of bread
  2. Heat up oil or butter in a pan at a medium-low heat
  3. Place the sandwich in the pan
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side
  5. Carefully remove the sandwich from the pan then serve.
